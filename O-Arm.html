The O-arm technology combined with StealthStation S8 Software are tools in the surgical suite that help ensure a safe, efficient, and more comfortable surgical experience for our patients. All instruments are visualized by the machines cameras so that a provider can place hardware using 3D imagery. This minimizes any injuries that can be caused by misplaced hardware and allows for minimally invasive fusion surgeries. Essentially it allows the doctor to work through tiny holes since they see everything on the screen and not directly thought he incision.
