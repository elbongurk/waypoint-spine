Thank you Waypoint spine, Dr Fairchild and Cavanaugh for giving me my life back!

A.R.
2019

Severe right leg pain had made it impossible to walk any distance. The problem was nerve related as a result of severe lumbar spine compression. After exhausting all the non-surgical alternatives, I realized my back was a mess and it was time to find a real spine specialist - someone with experience who works on backs every day. I was fortunate to find Dr Potocki. He came highly recommended and had just joined Dr. Fairchild at Waypoint Spine in Stevens Point. I was probably one of their first patients. Dr Potocki and his team performed a very complicated seven hour Posterior Lumbar Decompression and Spinal Fusion surgery on me in November of 2018. My surgery was a success and I am able to walk, play golf and live my very active life pain-free. Dr. Potocki’s skill as a surgeon is complimented by the follow-up care from his nurse Becca and Physical Therapist Amy. Their professionalism, knowledge and compassion made Linda and I both feel very comfortable through the entire surgical experience. Dr Potocki gave me my life back – a second chance to walk pain-free and be active again.

J.W.

2019
