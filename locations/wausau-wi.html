Wausau, WI

Dr. Todd A. Fairchild is the orthopedic spine surgeon directing our Wausau location. The Wausau office will be Dr. Fairchild's primary location and is easily accessible and conveniently located on Rib Mountain Drive, across the street from Barnes and Noble bookstore. In addition to appointment times with Dr. Fairchild, the Wausau location will offer X-ray, MRI, and physical therapy services.

Hospital Partnerships

We have partnered with many of the major area hospitals including the Ascension and Aspirus networks along with the Stevens Point Ambulatory Surgical Center which allows our providers to perform surgeries and other procedures at locations that both maximize patient convenience and insurance cooperation.
