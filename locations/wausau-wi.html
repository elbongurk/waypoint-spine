Dr. Todd A. Fairchild

Wausau, WI — Coming in January 2019

Todd A. Fairchild, M.D., is an orthopedic spine surgeon at Waypoint Spine. Dr. Fairchild founded Waypoint Spine in Stevens Point in 2016. While he will continue to see patients in the Stevens Point clinic, Dr. Fairchild is excited to announce the opening of a Wausau clinic in January 2019.

The Wausau office will be Dr. Fairchild's primary location and is easily accessible and conveniently located on Rib Mountain Drive, across the street from Barnes and Noble bookstore. In addition to appointment times with Dr. Fairchild, the Wausau location will offer X-ray, MRI, and physical therapy services.
