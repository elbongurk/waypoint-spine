Michael W. Cavanaugh, PA-C

Mike is an orthopedic spine Physician Assistant at Waypoint Spine. He grew up in Wisconsin Rapids and graduated from Lincoln High School. Mike received his Bachelor of Science Degree in Chemistry from UW-Eau Claire in 1988 and went on to receive his Master's Degree in Chemistry from Purdue University in 1990. He was employed in the paper industry as a technical service chemist for 11 years before returning to college to pursue a new career as a physician assistant. Mike is a member of the Wisconsin Association of Physician Assistants, the American Association of Physician Assistants and the North American Spine Society. He is an avid cross-country skier who skis the American Birkebeiner each winter and enjoys teaching middle school skiers in Wausau's Night Gliders ski program.
