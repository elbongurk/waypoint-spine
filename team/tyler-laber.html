Tyler P. Laber, PA-C

Tyler Laber has worked as a Physician Assistant since 2012. He has served in the Army National Guard since 2001, deploying previously to Iraq and Afghanistan. In addition to his civilian career, he continues to serve as a PA-C in the Wisconsin Army National Guard.

Upon completing their schooling, Tyler and his wife, Tessa, moved to Wausau in 2012. They both grew up in Wisconsin and enjoy being outdoors taking in the many opportunities living in north-central Wisconsin offers.
