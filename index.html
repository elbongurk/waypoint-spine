Waypoint Spine is an orthopedic practice dedicated exclusively to evaluating and treating disorders of the cervical, thoracic, and lumbar spine.

Panel 1

About Waypoint Spine

Our sole interest is caring for the spine. Every person in our practice has the experience, understanding, and commitment necessary for the spine-specific care of our patients. Simply worded, spine is all we do.

We believe that successful treatment can often be achieved non-surgically. If surgery is necessary, our patients receive expert care that emphasizes sound judgement, technical expertise, and compassion.

Panel 2

Our Services

Spinal Imaging

Waypoint Spine offers convenient on-site plain X-ray and MRI imaging.

Physical Therapy

Our spine-specific physical therapists work one-on-one with patients, establishing treatment plans that help avoid surgery when possible or recover from surgery if needed. Treatment plans include individualized exercises in our full physical therapy gym and/or additional therapy modalities, such as ultrasound and dry-needling.

Surgical Treatment

Our orthopedic spine surgeons are skilled in procedures to treat the cervical, thoracic, and lumbar spine, including:

  • microdiscectomies
  • laminectomies
  • spinal fusions
  • disc replacements
  • minimally invasive techniques
  • motion preserving surgical techniques
  • kyphoplasty/vertebroplasty

Neurology

While our team currently collaborates with outside neurologists when needed, Waypoint Spine anticipates future on-site neurology services, such as headache care and electromyography (EMG). For updates, check our website or call our office at (715) 544-1775.
