Waypoint Spine is transitioning its resources and referrals to a new practice:

PREMIER SPINE GROUP, S.C.

Opening in September 16, 2024, Premier Spine Group will feature the experience and skill of two orthopedic spine surgeons with deep roots in Central Wisconsin: Dr. William Bodemer and Dr. Todd Fairchild.



Dr. Bodemer grew up in Wausau and has practiced in Appleton for the past 8 years.

Dr. Todd Fairchild practiced in the Wausau and Stevens Point area for 15 years before joining Dr. Bodemer in Appleton for the past 18 months.

Drs. Bodemer and Fairchild look forward to making Stevens Point the home for their new practice and invite you to call (715) 544-1775 to make an appointment for a spine treatment evaluation.
