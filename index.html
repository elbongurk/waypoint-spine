Waypoint Spine is an orthopedic practice dedicated exclusively to the evaluation and treatment of disorders of the cervical, thoracic, and lumbar spine.

Panel 1

About Waypoint Spine

We believe that successful treatment can often be achieved non-surgically. If surgery is necessary, patients should receive expert care that emphasizes sound judgement, technical expertise, and compassion.

Our sole interest is caring for the spine. Every person in our practice has the experience, understanding, and commitment necessary for the spine-specific care of our patients. Simply worded, spine care is the only thing we do.

Panel 2

Our Services

Spinal Imaging

Both locations include on site plain x-ray and MRI imaging.

Physical Therapy

Both locations include a full physical therapy gym. Coming in 2019, the Stevens Point location will offer heated pools for aquatic therapy.

Surgical Treatment

Surgical treatment of the cervical, thoracic, and lumbar spine including microdiscectomies, laminectomies, spinal fusions, disc replacements, minimally invasive techniques, motion preserving surgical techniques, and kyphoplasty/vertebroplasty.
